## 1sh Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 13.05.2026 (13th May 2026)

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what data we (Maciej Gomoła, the operator of 1sh) collect, why, and how we use it.

---

### 1\. What We Collect

We may collect or receive:

* The long URL you submit when creating a short link
* Account information if you register: email address, display name, and (if using GitHub login) your GitHub profile data
* Click analytics for shortened links: country (derived from Cloudflare headers), HTTP referer, user agent, and a daily-rotating SHA-256 hash of your IP address used only for click deduplication — the raw IP is never stored
* Anti-spam verification data via Cloudflare Turnstile

---

### 2\. Authentication & Accounts

Account creation is optional. If you register, we store your email, display name, hashed password (email/password login) or OAuth provider token (GitHub login), and session data. Sessions are managed via **Better Auth** and stored in our PostgreSQL database.

Registered users can view, edit, and delete their own shortened links and access per-link click analytics.

---

### 3\. Hosting & Infrastructure

1sh runs on a dedicated server hosted in **France** (European Union). All data — including your account information, shortened links, and analytics — is stored on this server.

We use **Cloudflare** as a reverse proxy and DDoS protection layer. Cloudflare may process your IP address and request metadata. See [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

We are **no longer hosted on Vercel**, and Vercel no longer processes any data related to this service.

---

### 4\. Analytics

1sh runs its own privacy-respecting click analytics, stored in our PostgreSQL database. When someone visits a short link, we record:

* Country (from Cloudflare's `cf-ipcountry` header — no geolocation lookup performed)
* HTTP referer
* User agent string
* A daily-rotating SHA-256 hash of the visitor's IP address, used solely to deduplicate clicks within a 24-hour window — this hash cannot be reversed to recover the original IP

Analytics are visible **only** to the owner of the short link (when logged in) and to administrators. No analytics are shared with third parties.

---

### 5\. Cookies

We use only **strictly necessary cookies** required for the service to function:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `better-auth.session_token` | Authenticates your logged-in session | Session / persistent (up to 30 days) |

No advertising, tracking, or third-party analytics cookies are set by 1sh.

Cloudflare may set cookies as part of its proxy and bot-mitigation functionality (e.g. `__cf_bm`, `cf_clearance`). These are strictly necessary for network security. See [Cloudflare's Cookie Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/cookie-policy/).

Because we set only strictly necessary cookies, no cookie consent banner is required.

---

### 6\. Data Retention

| Data | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Shortened links (no account) | **2 years** from creation date, then automatically expired |
| Shortened links (registered user) | Until deleted by the owner or account deletion |
| Account data | Until account is deleted |
| Click analytics (link events) | **2 years** from the event date |
| Server and application logs | **1 month**, then automatically purged |
| Session tokens | Up to 30 days, or until sign-out |

---

### 7\. Domain Blacklist

If abuse is reported and confirmed by us, a domain associated with an abusive URL may be added to the domain blacklist. This results in:

* All existing short links pointing to that domain being disabled and removed
* Permanent prevention of new short links to that domain being created

If you believe your domain was blacklisted in error, you may file a removal request at **contact@1sh.pl** with supporting information. We will review and respond in a reasonable timeframe.

---

### 8\. Your Rights (GDPR)

As the server is located in France (EU), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies. You have the right to:

* **Access** personal data we hold about you
* **Rectify** inaccurate data
* **Erase** your account and associated data
* **Object** to or **restrict** processing
* **Data portability**

To exercise these rights, contact us at **contact@1sh.pl**.

---

### 9\. Deletion Requests

Registered users can delete their own short links directly from the dashboard. To delete your account or request removal of data you do not have direct access to, contact **contact@1sh.pl**.

For anonymous short links (created without an account), we cannot verify ownership. Abuse reports can be submitted to **contact@1sh.pl**.

---

### 10\. Legal Jurisdiction

This service is operated by **Maciej Gomoła** in **Poland**. The server is located in **France** (European Union). Applicable law includes Polish law and relevant EU regulations including the GDPR.

---

### 11\. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted here with an updated "Effective Date."
